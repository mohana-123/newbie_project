"VIGNAN"

Vignan's Institute of Information Technology was established in 2002 Visakhapatnam,to cater the educational needs of the public. It acquired Autonomous status in the year 2017,NBA and NAAC(A+) accredited Institute. The experience gained by Vignan through its earlier institutions at Guntur has only helped in establishing very high quality standards right from the inception.Vignan's Institute of Information Technology was established by Dr Lavu Rathaiah in the year 2002 in Visakhapatnam. The institute is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education and is affiliated to the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada.
